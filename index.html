About

The Scores is a 6 piece modern a capella group founded in September 2012 by graduates of the Royal Academy of Music and the Royal College of Music. Based in London, we are bookable for concerts, events, workshops and business functions. Bringing you an array of music ranging from current chart hits to 1970s soul, we love to entertain all audiences, and wherever possible get them involved too!

If you have any enquiries about bookings or indeed requests of songs you would like to see us perform then get in touch.

Bios

Annija Putnina

Our smooth soprano Annie is from a classically trained background coming all the way from Latvia She is no one trick pony, however, with an amazing ability to sing all styles of music from jazz to pop to vocal synth...! It is no surprise really given her previous experience as the leading lady of one of Latvia’s biggest a capella groups.

Kristin Lidström

The absolute best Swede in the group, bringing a gigantic range that could surely only bettered by Mariah Carey. She provides the bottom notes when the boys are feeling a bit tired and yet has a cracking high belt for a little one. We think it might owe to her bizarre ‘goat-like’ warm-ups but no one will truly know.

Alex Spinney

Known in the group as the ‘riff master’, Alex is a music graduate from Cambridge, whose ridiculous vocal technique has earned him the role as the front man of the group. Alex sings pretty much everywhere, and basically all the time, but why wouldn’t you if you have a voice that can reel in so much attention.

Luke Osborne

This classy Leeds graduate provides that silky floating pop voice that most people envy. Having been in a barbershop group before, Luke adapted quickly to the group turning his doo waps into trumpets and guitar sounds. A slick mover, a tight blender and also giving us some soulful solos Luke is a vital piece of the puzzle.

Joe Vetch

Wherever Joe goes, a capella goes. Having been in 4 a capella groups in as many years, Joe set up the group hoping from some permanent a capella fun in London. Singing bass in the group, and providing the quirky arrangements, Joe combines with Mark to form a solid foundation to the groove.

Mark Nathan

As a first study operatice bass with a barbershop background, how Mark acquired his spectacular beatboxing talents is beyond many. A founding member of the ‘Sons of Pitches’, Mark met Joe through their a capella endeavors and thought it only logical to continue the partnership in the scores. With the crazy ability to beatbox and sing two parts all at the same time, the rest of us wonder sometimes why we’re needed.
